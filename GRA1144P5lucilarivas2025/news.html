GRIZ RETURNING TO MUSIC AFTER INDEFINITE HIATUS:

"WE ARE SO BACK"

After nearly two years of silence that left fans in limbo, GRiZ has emerged from his creative hibernation to announce new 

THREE-DAY MUSIC FESTIVAL,

SEVEN STARS

After an indefinite hiatus that felt like an eternity to his legion of funk-loving fans, GRiZ has announced a new three-day music festival, Seven Stars, and its inaugural lineup. The event is scheduled for October 10-12 in Arrington, Virginia, just south of Charlottesville. The beloved DJ, producer and saxophonist, whose career has spanned over a decade of genre-bending releases and festival headlines, describes Seven Stars as "a reflection of my life's work" and "a culmination of everything I've wanted to share." 

 GRiZ will perform three sets at the first-ever Seven Stars. His curated lineup features a total of 67 artists, including Subtronics, Of The Trees, Levity, Hamdi, INZO, Wooli, Mary Droppinz, Maddy O'Neal, Disco Lines, Dr. Fresch, LYNY, Ravenscoon and EDM.com Class of 2025 stars Tape B and ALLEYCVT, among others.
