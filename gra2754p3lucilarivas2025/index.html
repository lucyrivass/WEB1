Beautiful DIY Wooden Lanterns

What you need:
  • (4) 1 in. square dowels @ 14 in. (sides)
  • (8) 1 in. square dowels @ 7 in. (cross supports)
  • 1 x 10 boards
  • (1) 1 x 10 @ 9½ in. (base)

STEP 1: Make Side Panels

Attach 2 cross supports to 2 sides. Use wood glue and 2 inch finish nails. Make 2 panels.

STEP 2: Attach Cross Supports

Attach 4 cross supports to one side panel using wood glue and 2 inch finish nails.

STEP 3: Attach Final Side Panel

Attach the other side panel to complete the box shape. Use wood glue and 2 inch finish nails.

STEP 4: Attach Base

Attach the baseusing wood glue and 1¼" countersunk wood screws

Final result

Finalize by fitting candle inside your beautiful frame

Ready to use!

Take decoration to a whole new level with just 4 simple steps!
